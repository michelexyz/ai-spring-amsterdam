A key paper that demonstrated the usefulness of RL for eliciting reasoning capabilities in LLMs, trading compute for more data.
Importantly, they introduce a new efficinent RL algorithm, GRPO, building on OpenAI's PPO. This enabled the DeepSeek research team to train a model with capabilities close to the frontier with much less compute than US labs, giving a glimpse of hope to the rest of the world that it is possible to train SOTA models with limited resources.

Demonstrates how reinforcement learning (without supervised fine-tuning on chain-of-thought data) can elicit strong reasoning capabilities in LLMs. A key paper for understanding the current push toward reasoning-capable models.
