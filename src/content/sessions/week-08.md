For the last session, instead of going into the details of a specific paper, a subset of the participants will each bring a paper and give a short presentation about it (5 to 10 min). The presentations are aimed at sparking curiosity in the audience, so everyone can read the proposed picks during the summer.

🧃 We will also have catering and networking after!

🧑‍🏫 If you want to present a paper, you can apply [here](https://forms.gle/Ryk6dEbwRYfR4kPi9).
